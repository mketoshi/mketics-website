# MKETICS AI-Integrated Platform Roadmap

Website: https://mketics.co.za  
Company: MKETICS (PTY) LTD  
Roadmap Stage: Future dynamic platform planning  
Current Stable Release: v1.0.0

---

## 1. Vision

The future MKETICS platform should move beyond a static company website.

The goal is to build a dynamic business system that helps MKETICS:

- Capture leads
- Qualify enquiries
- Recommend services
- Generate quote direction
- Manage clients
- Track projects
- Handle support requests
- Produce proposals and documents
- Use AI safely to improve speed, structure and service quality

The platform must support MKETICS as both:

1. A public digital service provider
2. An internal operating system for managing clients and projects
